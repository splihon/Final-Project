Mocha (she/her)

Meet Mocha! Debuting in 2007, Mocha was introduced in the Cinnamaroll universe as a fashion designer. She loves to create outfits for all of her friends, especially Cinnamaroll, who she is rumored to be in a relationship with. Mocha is a special character, meaning she is rarely found in Sanrio stores or merchandise. Her plushies are hard to find and can cost a pretty penny on bidding websites. She is independent, kind, and creative.
