Chococat (he/his)

Chococat is a Sanrio character who is a very spunky cat who loves to play around!

Friendly reminder to enjoy the little things! -Chococat
You can never have too much chocolate! -Chococat

Debut: 1996

Gender: Male

Species: Black Cat

Star Sign: Taurus

Career: Detective

Favorite Treat: Chocolate chip cookie

Name Origin: Comes from his chocolate-colored nose.

Fun Fact: His eyes are white, a trait which sets him apart from most other Sanrio Characters.

Special talent: He's always up on the latest news, thanks to his ultra-sensitive "antenna" whiskers, but can be a little scatter-brained at times.
